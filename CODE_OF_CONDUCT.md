# Code of Conduct

As contributors and maintainers of this project, we are committed to fostering an open, welcoming, and harassment-free environment.

We pledge to:
- Be respectful and inclusive
- Provide constructive feedback
- Accept differing viewpoints
- Show empathy toward other community members

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainer at any time. All complaints will be reviewed and addressed fairly.
